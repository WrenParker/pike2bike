Pike 2 Bike

 

Abandoned.

 

In use for only 28 of its 130 years until 2001

Built late 19th century,

abandoned.

Used as part of America's 1st Super Highway, the Pennsylvania Turnpike, 1940 - 1968,

then abandoned.

Come, join us on America's most unique, and strange bike trail.

Visit a place and time that never existed.

America after the apocalypse!

Is this the past, or is it the future ?
